Rules of the Game

1. The object of Tic Tac Toe is to get three in a row.

2. You play on a three by three game board.

3. The first player is known as X and the second is O.

4. Players alternate placing Xs and Os on the game board until either oppent has three in a row or all nine squares are filled.

5. X always goes first, and in the event that no one has three in a row, the stalemate is called a cat game.
